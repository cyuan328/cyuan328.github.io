Yuan Cheng
Group Leader
Yuan Cheng is an Associate Professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Currently, he leads the Computing Technology Laboratory (CLab) at Shanghai Jiaotong University.

Visiting Scholar

Yuzhe Zhang
Research Interests: Computational Optics, Optical Computing, Computational Imaging and Sensing
Contact: yuzhezhang@connect.hku.hk
Yuzhe Zhang is a PhD student in the CLab at The University of Hong Kong, working in the Imaging Systems Laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Edmund Y. Lam. He received his Master's degree from the Beijing Institute of Technology and later worked at SenseTime as a researcher on the development of mobile intelligent vision models. He published multiple research papers in journals and conferences such as Nature, Advanced Materials, CVPR, etc. His research focuses on utilizing light–matter interactions to surpass the performance limits of conventional optical sensors and electronic processors, with the goal of enabling multidimensional sensing and efficient computing.
